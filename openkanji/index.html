OpenKanji is a free app for learning Japanese kanji — genuinely useful first, and free forever.

Features

  • Handwriting practice with on-device recognition — forgiving stroke order.
  • Draw a kanji to look it up.
  • Recognition quizzes and spaced-repetition reviews (SM-2).
  • Tap through sentences → words → kanji → radicals. Search any of them.
  • JLPT N5–N1, fully offline. No account.
